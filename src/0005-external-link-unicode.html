Why is there no external tab character in unicode?

Before creating this horseshit of a hack/mess in my CSS, I was optimistic. I believed that a character for "external link" must exist among 144,697 characters in unicode. Oh Boy was I wrong..

There are literally fuck tons of useless characters in unicode. And somehow there is not a character as useful and widely used as this. While trying to find something that at-least resembles the "external link" icon, I found this article : Why isn't the external link symbol in Unicode?

TLDR : In 2006, Someone submitted a well written proposal to add an "external link" character to unicode. The proposal was ignored for 6 years and was finally rejected in 2012.

Apperently the dicksuckers at unicode believe that "the external link icon is not an element of plain text". Bitch! emojis ain't an element of plain text either. But we still have them in unicode.

Extensions are useful

Just want to point this out first. Extensions makes the time I spend browsing less miserable. From removing useless features in Google search to bringing back the dislike count, there's an extension for everything.

But still some extension developer just want to make my already pathetic life more pathetic. Which brings me to my second point.

Extensions are fucking annoying

Please tell me why the fuck I need a welcome screen when I installed a fucking extension? why the fuck do you need to show me the damn changelogs on every update which I don't give a shit about? looking at you Wappalyzer..

A new tab is something that must be initiated on user's wish. This is something that a user controls and it should stay that way.

I just recently logged in to my chrome profile to get my extensions and bookmarks back. Almost 8 extensions decided to bomb the shit out of miserable laptop by showing their own shitty little welcome screen, in seperate tabs.

Every fucking time I want to concentrate and do some research. Some peice of shit extension decides to show me the update changelogs. I DON'T GIVE A SHIT ABOUT YOUR CHANGELOGS, MOTHERFUCKER!!.

And everytime I fucking uninstall some shitty extension.. BOOM! fucking survey page. This horseshit make me so fucking pissed, that sometimes I wish to shit in the dev's mouth.

I might be overreacting. But there are pleny non-obtrusive way to welcome your users, collect feedback or to show the damn changelogs.

I wish that browser provide a permission system to whitelist or "one time allow" the extension to open new tabs.
